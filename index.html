Metric Formulas
CT ROAS
Sum(Conv. Value) ÷ Sum(Spend)
Return on ad spend based on purchase conversion value
Hook Rate
Sum(3-Sec Plays) ÷ Sum(Impressions)
% of people who watched at least 3 seconds — measures creative hook strength
ThruPlay Rate
Sum(ThruPlays) ÷ Sum(Impressions)
% who watched the video to completion (or 15s for longer videos)
Hold Rate
Sum(ThruPlays) ÷ Sum(3-Sec Plays)
Of those hooked at 3s, % who stayed till the end — measures content retention
Outbound CTR
Sum(Outbound Clicks) ÷ Sum(Impressions)
Unique click-through rate to destination URL (matches Meta Ads Manager)
Engagement Rate
Sum(Post Engagements) ÷ Sum(Impressions)
Likes, comments, shares and saves as % of impressions
ℹ Data Deduplication
The sheet may contain multiple rows for the same Date + Ad ID combination due to sync overlaps. The dashboard automatically removes duplicates, keeping the latest entry per combination before all calculations. Rate metrics further exclude rows with 0 impressions from their denominators to match Meta Ads Manager's behaviour precisely.
Changes to thresholds apply instantly to the dashboard
