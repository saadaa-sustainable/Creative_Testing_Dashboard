Categorisation Algorithm
Each unique ad is evaluated against a 4-filter matrix. F1 is mandatory for Winner. Incremental Winners pass F1 + at least one of F2/F3 + F4. Winners pass F1 + at least one of F2/F3 (F4 not required). Ads that pass only F1 are sent to ITE Priority 2. Failed ads land in Analyse (with signal) or Discarded (no signal).

Metric Formulas
★ Cost / NCP (F3)
Σ(Spend) ÷ Σ(NCP Count)
Computed per unique ad across all filtered dates. ₹0 cost (no NCPs) = no F3 signal
★ CT ROAS (F2)
Sum(Conv. Value) ÷ Sum(Spend)
Purchase conversion value return on ad spend
Hook Rate
Sum(3-Sec Plays) ÷ Sum(Impressions)
% who watched at least 3 seconds — creative hook strength
ThruPlay Rate
Sum(ThruPlays) ÷ Sum(Impressions)
% who watched to completion or 15s for longer videos
Hold Rate
Sum(ThruPlays) ÷ Sum(3-Sec Plays)
Of those hooked at 3s, % who stayed till end
Outbound CTR
Sum(Outbound Clicks) ÷ Sum(Impressions)
Unique click-through rate to destination URL
ℹ Data Deduplication
Rows are deduplicated by Date + Ad ID before any calculation. Rate metrics exclude rows with 0 impressions from their denominators to match Meta Ads Manager. CPC uses Outbound Clicks only — an ad with 0 outbound clicks gets CPC = 0 and does NOT qualify for F3.
F2/F3 = ROAS ≥ target OR Cost/NCP ≤ target · Winner = F1 ✓ F2/F3 ✓ · Inc.Winner = F1 ✓ F2/F3 ✓ F4 ✓
